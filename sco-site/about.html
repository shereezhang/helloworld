Who we are

We're a team of seven Industrial Engineering students at the University of Toronto who have all, at some point, had the same question: "What is Industrial Engineering, and what can I do with it?"

That's the question this platform aims to solve. Whether you're a first year student looking into Indy courses or a student going into third-year trying to select electives, we have compiled reviews of all the core and technical elective courses from people who have previously gone through it all.

What's more, you can also help out future Industrial engineers by adding your own reviews!
